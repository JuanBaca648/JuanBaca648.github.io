Hi my name is Juan Baca Jimenez, I am an artist

Hi my name is Juan Baca Jimenez, and I am an aspiring artist born in San Jose, California. I was raised 30 minutes south of San Jose my whole life. I am enjoy doing photography and painting, and I recently became interested in the Digital Media Art World. I enjoy painting with acrylic on canvases. The painting genres I focus on are landscape, architecture, abstract paintings, still life. I have experience in creating scupltures and throwing clay and created small scuptures and bowls so I had the chance to get my hands dirty with clay. I don't want to limit myself, so I am eager to learn more about the digital aspect of Art and reach my potential.
